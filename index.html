About the Game

Your Feathery Journey Begins

The Universal Pigeon Service is an idle clicker game where you start with a single pigeon — each blessed with a unique, randomly generated government name — ready to tackle their delivery route.


Your pigeon works tirelessly to complete deliveries and earn seeds as payment. Spend those seeds on better training, faster factories, and new birds. Build your flock, optimize your routes, and rise to become the most elite UPS carrier the world has ever seen.

Game Highlights

What makes UPS special

🏋️

Pigeon Stat Upgrades

Train your pigeons across running, climbing, and flying to watch them blaze through delivery routes faster than ever.

🏭

Box Factory Upgrades

Deliveries require boxes! Upgrade your factory to keep a steady supply of packages flowing to your hard-working birds.

Expand Your Flock

Accumulate enough seeds to recruit new pigeons, each specializable for different route conditions and delivery types.

📋

Government Names

Each pigeon receives a randomly generated full name — making your flock memorable, and occasionally hiding a fun easter egg.
